Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from regular consulting?

Consulting is typically project-based and transactional—you get a deliverable, then you're on your own. Fractional COO is ongoing partnership. I'm in your business every week, reviewing KPIs, solving problems in real-time, and holding you accountable to execution. Think of it as having a COO on retainer, not a consultant on a project.

What if I already completed a Profit Leak Snapshot with you?

Perfect—that means we've already identified your top profit leaks and priority fixes. The Fractional COO partnership is where we execute on those recommendations together. Plus, your $750 Snapshot fee gets credited toward your first month if you start within 30 days.

Can I cancel after the 3-month minimum?

Yes. After the initial 3-month commitment, the engagement is month-to-month. You can pause or cancel with 30 days notice. Most clients stick around because they see the value—but there's no long-term lock-in beyond the first 90 days.

Do you work on-site or is this remote?

This is primarily remote partnership—weekly Zoom calls and Slack/email support. If you're within driving distance of Central Virginia and need an on-site visit for a specific project (new location opening, training session, etc.), we can arrange that for an additional fee.

What kind of results can I expect?

Most clients see measurable improvements in KPIs (labor %, food cost, sales trends) within the first 60 days. Beyond the numbers, you'll get clarity on priorities, accountability on execution, and strategic confidence in your decisions. Think of it as getting your time back to focus on growth instead of firefighting, plus an experienced operator to gut-check your big moves.
