Advisory / Fractional Ops

Your operator-grade partner for strategic execution. Weekly KPI reviews, hands-on problem-solving, and accountability to keep your operations moving forward.

The Problem You're Facing

You're wearing too many hats—operator, marketer, HR, finance, strategist. You know what needs to be done, but execution keeps slipping. Important projects drag on for months. KPIs aren't moving. Your team needs direction, but you're buried in daily fires. You can't afford a full-time COO, but you desperately need operator-level strategic support.

The cost? Stalled growth, missed opportunities, inconsistent execution, and the constant feeling that you're spinning your wheels instead of building momentum.

What Fractional Ops Leadership Delivers

Strategic Partner, Not Consultant

Weekly calls, ongoing access, and real accountability. Not drive-by advice—hands-on partnership that keeps projects moving and KPIs improving.

KPI-Driven Focus

Define the 5-7 metrics that actually matter. Weekly reviews keep you focused on outcomes, not activity. Move the numbers that move your business.

Operator-Grade Problem Solving

Get answers from someone who's been in your shoes. No academic theory—practical solutions from 15+ years running multi-unit operations.

Execution Accountability

Set priorities, assign owners, track progress. Stop letting important projects drift. Regular cadence ensures follow-through.

Strategic Initiatives

Plan expansion, optimize operations, build systems, train leaders. Work on your business, not just in it—with expert guidance every step.

Fraction of Full-Time Cost

Get VP/COO-level strategic support for a fraction of a full-time salary. Scale up or down based on your needs and growth stage.

How Advisory Partnerships Work

Onboarding & Priority Setting

We start with a deep dive into your business—operations, financials, team, systems. We identify the 3-5 highest-impact areas and set clear 90-day objectives with measurable KPIs.

Weekly Strategic Calls

45-60 minute calls every week to review KPIs, solve problems, unblock projects, and set priorities for the coming week. Structured agenda, clear action items, documented decisions.

Ongoing Access & Support

Slack/email access between calls for urgent questions, quick decisions, and real-time problem-solving. You're not waiting a week when something critical comes up.

Hands-On Projects

Beyond strategy calls, I roll up sleeves on specific initiatives—building systems, training managers, designing processes, analyzing data. Not just telling you what to do—helping you execute.

Quarterly Planning & Reviews

Every 90 days, we step back to review progress, celebrate wins, adjust strategy, and set the next quarter's priorities. Keep long-term vision aligned with short-term execution.

Real Outcomes You Can Expect

After partnering with fractional ops leadership, clients typically see:

  • Clear strategic direction—no more spinning wheels on what to prioritize
  • Improved KPIs across labor, food cost, sales, and guest satisfaction
  • Projects that actually finish—systems implemented, processes documented, initiatives launched
  • Better decision-making—operator-tested frameworks for common challenges
  • Team alignment—managers clear on priorities and accountable for results
  • Faster growth—expansion plans executed, new locations opened successfully
  • Your time back—fewer fires, more focus on leadership and strategy

Typical ROI: 5-10x return through margin improvement, strategic initiatives, and avoided mistakes—not counting the value of your time freed up to focus on growth instead of firefighting.

Who This Is For

This service is perfect for:

  • Multi-unit operators (3-15 locations) who need strategic ops leadership
  • Single-location owners planning expansion and need guidance
  • Operators without a strong #2 who need experienced operational support
  • Growing brands at inflection points (new markets, new concepts, scaling challenges)
  • Private equity-backed brands that need experienced operators to hit growth targets

Not a fit if: You're looking for one-time consulting, or you need someone on-site full-time. This is strategic partnership for operators who execute—not someone to run your daily operations for you.
