Local Store Marketing (LSM)

Stop throwing money at ads that don't work. Build a 12-week marketing calendar with trackable in-store activations, community partnerships, and offers that actually fill seats.

The Problem You're Facing

You're running random promotions with no clear strategy—$5 off Tuesday here, a Facebook post there, maybe a loyalty punch card someone mentioned. Marketing feels reactive instead of strategic. You're not sure what's working, what's wasting money, or how to drive traffic during slow periods. Meanwhile, competitors are consistently packed.

The cost? Inconsistent traffic, unpredictable revenue, wasted marketing spend on tactics that don't convert, and empty seats during periods you desperately need to fill.

What Strategic Local Marketing Delivers

Consistent Traffic

12-week marketing calendar with scheduled activations every week—no more scrambling for last-minute ideas. Predictable promotions = predictable traffic.

Trackable Results

Every offer has a unique code or tracking mechanism. Know exactly what drives traffic, what doesn't, and where to double down.

Community Integration

Build relationships with local businesses, schools, sports teams, and organizations. Turn your neighbors into your best marketing channel.

Fill Slow Periods

Strategic offers targeted at your specific slow days/dayparts. Turn dead Mondays into profitable service periods.

Guest Retention

Loyalty programs, email campaigns, and remarketing that bring customers back monthly instead of once a year.

Low-Cost, High-Impact

Focus on tactics with proven ROI—community partnerships, targeted offers, referral programs. Not expensive brand advertising.

How We Build Your LSM System

Traffic Analysis & Opportunity Mapping

We analyze your sales data to identify slow periods, peak times, and customer frequency patterns. We map your local area for partnership opportunities—businesses, schools, organizations within 3 miles.

12-Week Marketing Calendar

We build a 12-week calendar with specific activations every week—community nights, targeted offers, partnership promotions, loyalty rewards. Each activation has clear objectives and tracking.

Offer Design & Tracking Setup

We design offers that fill your specific needs (slow dayparts, new customer acquisition, repeat visits). Every offer has unique tracking codes so you know exactly what's working.

Partnership Development

We help you establish 3-5 local partnerships—corporate lunch programs, school fundraisers, sports team sponsorships. These become consistent monthly traffic drivers.

Execution Playbooks

We create simple execution guides for each activation—who does what, when to promote, how to track results. Your team can run these without constant oversight.

Monthly Optimization Reviews

We meet monthly to review performance, adjust tactics, and plan the next quarter's calendar. Double down on what works, kill what doesn't.

Real Outcomes You Can Expect

After implementing strategic local marketing, clients typically see:

  • 15-25% increase in traffic during previously slow periods
  • 30-40% improvement in customer frequency (visits per year)
  • 3-5 predictable partnerships driving monthly catering or group business
  • Trackable ROI on every promotion—know exactly what's worth repeating
  • Consistent weekly activations instead of reactive, random promotions
  • 10-15% reduction in paid advertising spend (replaced with higher-ROI tactics)
  • Community presence that generates word-of-mouth and organic traffic

Typical ROI: 5-8x return on marketing investment within the first quarter through increased traffic, better customer retention, and strategic partnerships—not counting the long-term value of community integration.

Who This Is For

This service is perfect for:

  • Single or multi-unit restaurants with inconsistent traffic patterns
  • Neighborhood concepts that need deeper community integration
  • Operators with significant slow periods (weekday lunch, Monday dinner, etc.)
  • Brands spending on ads without clear ROI or tracking
  • Growing concepts ready to build repeatable marketing systems

Not a fit if: You're already at max capacity every service period, or you're looking for brand awareness campaigns instead of traffic-driving tactics with measurable ROI.
