Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. When you submit your information through our contact form, we collect the details you provide (such as your name, email, company and any message you write) solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Our contact form posts to FormSubmit, a third‑party service that forwards your submission to our chosen inbox. FormSubmit may temporarily process your data on their servers in order to deliver the email. You can review their privacy policy on their website.

We also embed a scheduling widget from Calendly for your convenience. Any information you provide directly in the Calendly widget is processed by Calendly subject to their own privacy policy.

We may collect anonymous usage data (such as page visits) to improve this site. We use no advertising cookies or trackers.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us via the details on our contact page.
