Profit Leak Snapshot

Your operation is profitable on paper, but the money is not showing up in your account. Something is leaking.

The Profit Leak Snapshot is a 90-minute diagnostic session to identify your top 3 profit leaks, estimate what they are costing you, and decide what to fix first.

Investment: $750 (paid at booking)

What this is

This is not consulting theater. It is a fast, operator-level diagnosis.

In 90 minutes, we will:

  • Review your last 90 days of key numbers (P&L, labor summary, sales mix basics)
  • Pressure-test what you think the problem is
  • Identify the top 3 likely profit leaks
  • Estimate monthly and annual impact ranges
  • Prioritize what to fix first based on impact vs effort
  • Call out what not to touch yet

What you walk away with

You will leave the call with:

  • Your top 3 profit leaks, clearly named
  • A rough $ impact estimate for each leak (monthly and annual ranges)
  • A simple Fix First order: first, second, third
  • A clear recommendation on whether deeper work is worth it
Important: This is a diagnostic. It is designed to create clarity, not deliver a full implementation plan.

What this is not

To keep the Snapshot clean and valuable, we do not:

  • Rebuild schedules or staffing plans
  • Rewrite your menu or fully engineer it
  • Create SOPs
  • Set up software or AI automations
  • Deliver a recording
  • Deliver a written roadmap

If you want help implementing the fixes, that is a separate scope.

Who this is for

This is for operators who are:

  • Actually running the operation—not just thinking about it
  • Looking at P&Ls that don't match the effort going in
  • Ready to see the numbers behind the problem, not just talk in circles
  • Willing to spend $750 to stop guessing and start fixing

If you are exploring options, comparing consultants, or hoping for free advice, this is not the right fit. This is a paid diagnostic for operators who need clarity now.

What to bring

Have these ready on the call:

  • Last 3 months of P&L
  • A labor summary (hours, payroll, OT if available)
  • Basic sales mix or top sellers list from your POS (if available)

If you are missing something, we will work with what you have and flag the gaps.

Why this works

Most hospitality operations do not have one big problem. They have a few small leaks that compound.

The Snapshot helps you:

  • Stop chasing the wrong fix
  • See the cost in dollars, not feelings
  • Make one decision at a time, in the right order

You are buying clarity. Clarity drives profit.
