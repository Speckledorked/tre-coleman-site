SOPs & Training Systems

Eliminate tribal knowledge and get new hires productive 50% faster. Build training systems with role scorecards, checklists, micro-training, and 30-60-90 plans that actually stick.

The Problem You're Facing

Your best employees hold critical knowledge in their heads—opening procedures, quality standards, troubleshooting steps—that disappears when they're not on shift. New hires take 4-6 weeks to get productive, and even then, execution varies wildly between shifts. Managers spend hours re-training the same basics because nothing is documented.

The cost? Slow ramp times, inconsistent quality, frustrated managers training the same thing repeatedly, and customers experiencing different service depending on who's working.

What Professional SOPs & Training Deliver

50% Faster Onboarding

New hires follow clear checklists and micro-training videos. They hit productivity benchmarks in 2-3 weeks instead of 4-6, with less manager hand-holding.

Consistent Execution

Every team member follows the same proven process—opening, closing, service standards, quality checks. No more "that's not how I was trained."

Knowledge Transfer

Capture your best employees' expertise in simple, actionable SOPs. When they leave or get promoted, their knowledge stays with you.

Manager Leverage

Managers stop repeating the same training. They can point to a video or checklist instead of explaining from scratch every time.

Performance Clarity

Role scorecards define exactly what "good" looks like for each position. Performance conversations become objective, not subjective.

Scalable Standards

Built to scale across locations. Train new stores faster, maintain brand consistency, and reduce quality variability.

How We Build Your Training Systems

Role & Task Analysis

We shadow your team for 3-5 days, documenting every critical task, decision point, and quality standard. We identify your star performers and capture what makes them successful.

SOP Documentation

We create clear, step-by-step SOPs for every key process—opening/closing, prep work, service standards, cleaning, quality checks. Simple checklists, not 50-page manuals nobody reads.

Micro-Training Videos

We film 2-5 minute training videos for critical tasks using your actual team and equipment. New hires can watch, practice, and reference these any time.

Role Scorecards

We build role scorecards that define success metrics for each position—line cook, server, shift lead, etc. Clear expectations = objective performance reviews.

30-60-90 Day Plans

We create progressive training plans that gradually increase responsibility. New hires know exactly what they should master each month, and managers can track their progress.

Rollout & Training

We train your managers on how to use the new systems, implement them with your team, and provide 30 days of support to refine based on real-world feedback.

Real Outcomes You Can Expect

After implementing professional SOPs and training systems, clients typically see:

  • 50% faster onboarding—new hires productive in 2-3 weeks instead of 4-6
  • 40% reduction in manager time spent on repetitive training
  • Consistent quality across all shifts and locations
  • 20-30% reduction in early turnover (first 90 days)
  • Objective performance management—clear expectations eliminate disputes
  • Knowledge preservation—star employee expertise captured permanently
  • Faster multi-unit expansion—replicate training systems to new locations

Typical ROI: 4-6x return within the first 90 days through reduced training time, lower early turnover, and improved labor efficiency—not counting improved consistency and customer satisfaction.
